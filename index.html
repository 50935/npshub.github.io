Nepal Physical Society
School of Computing

Educational transformation in modern days requires engagement of multidisciplinary people.

We are pushing the boundary of Computation and Artificial Intelligence (AI) everyday. Our participants from the every corner of the world are joining to our mission of computational transformation and data awareness in Physics Education. Computation has matured into subdisciplines that have yielded a constellation of methods ranging from AI, smart governance, healthcare, security, education etc. Our attempt will be connecting people in Physics from every corner of the world to the common ground of Nepal Physical Society. We hope the valuable assessment, and guidance can be developed through focused studies, monitoring and analysis in our projects and implement them in the Physics Education. Let's unite to bring the Computational revolution!
